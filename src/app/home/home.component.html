What is Teach a Parent?

Teach a Parent is a webinar series by Students to teach the elderly. The webinar serries covers a series of topics related to Tech which might be helpful for parents in terms of their ward or well being.

Currently its happening with the help of students from Coimbatore. Later on, we have plans to implement it in a national level.
